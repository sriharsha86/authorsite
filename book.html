Poetry book
Is It Too Late To Heal?

“Is it too late to Heal?” is the raw and emotionally charged debut poetry collection about... the life of the Author and how she went past her trauma and embarked on the journey of Healing. Divyanshi delves into the intricate layers of the human experience with profound insight. Through her poetry, she fearlessly navigates themes of childhood trauma, abuse, grief, mental health struggles, love, loss, some sensitive themes like Sexual assault, suicidal ideation, other potentially traumatic experiences, and ultimately, the process of healing. Her words possess the power to unearth buried emotions and memories, evoking a deep sense of empathy and reflection in her readers.
